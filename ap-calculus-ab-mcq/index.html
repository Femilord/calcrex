MCQ Strategies
🎯
Eliminate First
Use boundary conditions and logic to rule out wrong answers before solving. Often you can eliminate 2 choices quickly.
🔌
Plug & Check
Substitute answer choices back into the problem. Especially powerful for antiderivatives and differential equations.
⚠️
Watch for Traps
Common traps include sign errors, forgetting the chain rule, and confusing the FTC parts. Read every answer carefully.
📐
Know FTC Cold
The Fundamental Theorem of Calculus appears in many forms. Be fluent with both Part 1 and Part 2.
📊
Use Your Calculator
In Part B, use your graphing calculator to graph, compute integrals, and verify answers. Don't guess when you can compute.
⏱️
Pace Yourself
Part A: ~2 min per question. Part B: ~3 min per question. Skip hard ones and return — no penalty for guessing.
