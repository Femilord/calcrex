Common Physics Formulas

Kinematics

  • v = d/t - Velocity
  • a = Δv/t - Acceleration
  • s = v₀t + ½at² - Displacement
  • v² = v₀² + 2as - Final velocity

Dynamics

  • F = ma - Newton's 2nd Law
  • W = Fd cos θ - Work
  • P = W/t - Power
  • KE = ½mv² - Kinetic Energy

Electricity

  • V = IR - Ohm's Law
  • P = VI - Electric Power
  • R_s = R₁ + R₂ - Series
  • 1/R_p = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ - Parallel

Waves & Optics

  • v = fλ - Wave speed
  • n₁ sin θ₁ = n₂ sin θ₂ - Snell's Law
  • 1/f = 1/u + 1/v - Lens formula
  • m = v/u - Magnification
