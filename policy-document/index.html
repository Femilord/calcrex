Cookie Policy

4.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and understand how you interact with it.

4.2 Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic website functionality (e.g., remembering your mobile menu state). These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Used by Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage statistics such as page views, session duration, and traffic sources. These help us understand which calculators are most useful.
  • Advertising cookies: Used by Google AdSense and its partners to serve relevant advertisements. These cookies may track your browsing activity across other websites to deliver targeted ads.

4.3 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect your experience on CalcREX and other websites.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

4.4 Google AdSense & Personalization

Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to CalcREX and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings or www.aboutads.info.
